Introductory

Where’s Waldo Game

By holding a profile of a person (kernel), align the profile image and scan it line by line. At each position, compare the image against the position in the picture (find out the similarity), which is checking the correlation between the input profile against the position.

  1. Translation invariance: We want to capture the pitch information
  2. Locality: We want to focus on the local regions first and don’t care about the meaning at the beginning. Aggregate them later on to know the meaning

Pooling Layers

It makes the representation smaller and more manageable by operate over each activation map independently
